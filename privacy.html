At CalcBeast (calcbeast.vercel.app), your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

Calculator Inputs: All values you enter into our calculators are processed entirely in your browser. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any numbers or data you enter into our calculator tools.

Contact Information: If you contact us via email, we may collect your name and email solely to respond to your inquiry.

Automatically Collected Data: We automatically collect technical information through log files and analytics tools including IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited, and time spent. This data is used in aggregate form to improve our website.

2. Cookies

  • Theme Preference: We store your light/dark mode preference in your browser's local storage.
  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics (via Google Tag Manager) to understand site usage. Data is anonymized and aggregated.
  • Advertising: We display Google AdSense ads which may use cookies to show relevant advertisements. You can opt out via Google's ad settings.

3. Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website uses: Google Analytics (traffic analytics), Google AdSense (advertising), Google Tag Manager (tag management), and Cloudflare CDN (performance). Each service has their own privacy policies governing data use.

5. Children's Privacy

CalcBeast does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

6. Data Security

Since we do not collect calculator input data, there is no sensitive financial or health data on our servers. Your calculations remain completely private on your own device.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to: access any personal data we hold, request deletion of your data, opt out of analytics cookies, and opt out of personalised advertising.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Contact us at: support@calcbeast.com
