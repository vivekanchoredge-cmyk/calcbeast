ℹ️ About This Calculator

This calculator combines SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) with flexible Lump Sum investments. Each lump sum you add in a specific month/year gets compounded using CAGR formula for the remaining period.

Formula Used

Monthly Rate (CAGR based): r = (1 + R/100)^(1/12) − 1

SIP Future Value: FV = P × [((1+r)^n − 1) / r] × (1+r)

Lump Sum: Compounded month-by-month using same r for remaining months

❓ FAQ

Can I add lump sum in any month?

Yes! You can specify exact year and month for each lump sum. Multiple lump sums in the same month are also supported.

What formula is used?

CAGR-based monthly compounding is used for all calculations.

Is SIP mandatory?

No. You can set SIP to 0 and use only lump sum investments.

What does extra months mean?

If your investment period is 10 years and 6 months, enter 10 in years and 6 in extra months.
